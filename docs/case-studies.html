Six Counties, Three Stories

The six case-study counties represent three distinct patterns relative to model predictions. Over-performers suggest that something in the civic environment — local organizing, competitive elections, cultural norms — is mobilizing voters beyond what demographics alone would predict. Near-expected counties serve as a baseline. Under-performers raise questions about suppression, disengagement, or structural barriers not captured by the model.

Each county profile includes:
(1) key statistics from the model,
(2) a comparison of predictors against the state average, and
(3) space for qualitative findings from document review, news archive analysis, or other sources.
